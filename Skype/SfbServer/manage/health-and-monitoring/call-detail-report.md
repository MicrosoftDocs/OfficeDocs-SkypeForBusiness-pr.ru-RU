<span data-ttu-id="022c5-p101">Следует помнить, что категории и метрики, которые можно видеть в данном отчете, зависят от типа сеанса и типа конечных точек, используемых в сеансе. Likewise, you might have a report that lists caller statistics but not callee statistics. That's typically because the callee was not using a SIP-compliant device. Endpoints are responsible for reporting statistics at the end of a call; however, a cell phone (which knows nothing about SIP or SIP statistics) is unable to report that kind of information. If you call someone and they answer you on their cell phone, you will not get a report from that cell phone when the call ends.</span></span>
